About Coaches

Welcome to our sports club, where wellness meets performance. We offer a variety of disciplines, including Salt Room Therapy, Stretching, Handstand Training, and Martial Arts. Our mission is to help you achieve your fitness goals in a supportive and inspiring environment.

Oleksandra Tabachynska is a Master of Sport International Class, multiple champion of Ukraine and four times European champion, as well as world champion. She is a world record holder and has been working as a coach for five years, teaching both children and adults. Oleksandra specialises in stretching, handstand, acrobatics and other sports disciplines.
