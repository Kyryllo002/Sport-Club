What is Salt Room Therapy?

Salt Room Therapy, also known as halotherapy, is a natural treatment that involves breathing in microscopic salt particles. This therapy is known for its numerous health benefits, including improved respiratory function, reduced stress, and enhanced skin health.

Benefits of Salt Room Therapy

  • Improves breathing and lung function.
  • Reduces stress and promotes relaxation.
  • Boosts the immune system.
  • Helps with skin conditions like eczema and psoriasis.
  • Enhances overall well-being.
